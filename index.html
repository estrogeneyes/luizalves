MNDR VR EXPERIENCE

Partnership with producer MNDR and Boise State University Department of Arts, to Create a VR experience for song "Dove".

    The concept we talked about for Dove derivers from the mysticality of opposites, and the polarizing pull both sides have on us. This experience is a series of visual choices the players make in order to transcend their way out of the brain maze.

Tapestry concept:

Amanda (MNDR) and I talked about creating lore from her previous music. We collectively shared the ideology of having two paths to be chosen and it is up to the player to decise which path to take. Each individual path gifts the player at the end a set of items which helps them go through the maze differently.

Students Interptretation:

The tapestry is a clue to the options the player has. They would have to decipher it and decice what path to ultimately take.

Maze Concept:

The psyche was a big early concept idea MNDR ( Amanda ) and I talked about. it is the origin of how we create our perception so naturally we would have to go in it to create this experience.

Students Interpretation:

Brain maze interpretation, with different levels within. The players will be able to unlock different passages within the experiences after collecting customizable items.
